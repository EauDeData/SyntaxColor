Lala Hsu (Chinese: 徐佳瑩; pinyin: Xú JiāYíng) is Taiwanese Mandopop artist Lala Hsu's (Chinese: 徐佳瑩) self-titled debut Mandarin solo studio album. It was released by Asiamuse on 29 May 2009. The album features 11 self-composed tracks. The album was the bestseller in 2009.[citation needed]
With this album, Lala Hsu won "Best New Artist" at the 21st Golden Melody Awards.
