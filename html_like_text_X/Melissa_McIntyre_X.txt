Melissa Adaleigh McIntyre (born May 31, 1986)  is a Canadian former actress. She is best known for her role as Ashley Kerwin on the long-running teen television series Degrassi: The Next Generation.[1]

Career[edit]
McIntyre was a participant in many community theatre plays as a child, including The Wizard of Oz and The Secret Garden.[2]
In 1998, she was cast in another television show, Real Kids, Real Adventures.[2]
McIntyre was also a radio actress, as she played the lead character in Disco Does Not Suck.[2] She took some time off during 1999 to participate mainly in theatre, but in 2000 McIntyre, then thirteen years old, returned to television, voicing the character "Cornflower" in the cartoon series Mattimeo: A Tale of Redwall.[2]
In 2001, she was cast in Degrassi: The Next Generation, a sequel to the popular 1980s series Degrassi High. McIntyre played Ashley Kerwin.[1]
