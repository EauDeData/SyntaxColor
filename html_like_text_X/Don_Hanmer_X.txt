Don Hanmer ( October 17, 1919  – May 24, 2003) was an American film actor. 
He started at Broadway, where he was considered once a big hit. He appeared in 90 films between 1945 until 1991.
He was born in Chicago Illinois, USA and died in Monterey California, USA from cancer. He was married to Jocelyn Brando and then later Susan Tucker Huntington [1]
