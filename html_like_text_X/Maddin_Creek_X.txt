	Maddin Creek
	
	
		From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
		
		
		
		
		
		Jump to navigation
		Jump to search
		Maddin Creek is a stream in Washington County in the U.S. state of Missouri.[1] It is a tributary of the Big River.
Maddin Creek, historically spelled "Madden Branch", has the name of the local Madden family.[2]
