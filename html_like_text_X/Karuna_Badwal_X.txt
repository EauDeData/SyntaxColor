Karuna BadwalBornBombay, India  (now Mumbai, Maharashtra)OccupationFilm producerSpouse(s)Ravinder Singh BadwalChildrenHans Badwal and Shums Badwal
Karuna Badwal is an Indian film producer; she has co-produced hit films like Chennai Express[1][2] and Happy New Year.[3]

Career[edit]
She started her film career as Shahrukh Khan's business manager.[4][5]

Personal life[edit]
She is married to Ravinder Singh Badwal and the couple have two children Hans Badwal, Shums Badwal
