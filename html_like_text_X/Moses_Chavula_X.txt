Moses Chavula (born 8 August 1985) is a Malawian footballer who plays as a defender for MTL Wanderers.
A longtime member of the Malawi national football team, Chavula has competed for his national team since 2003.[1] Chavula is a left-footed player and plays as left-back for his team and Malawi.
