State Road 254 (NM 254) is a 4.378-mile-long (7.046 km) state highway in the US state of New Mexico. NM 254's northern terminus is at U.S. Route 380 (US 380) in Roswell, and the southern terminus is at NM 256 in Roswell.

Major intersections[edit]
The entire route is in Roswell, Chaves County.
