Narciso Valentin Ticobay (19 March 1932-21 July 2013) was a Filipino Episcopalian bishop.[1] He was the second Prime Bishop the Episcopal Church in the Philippines from 1993 to 1997.[2] He also served as the second Diocesan Bishop the EDSP from 1986-1993.[2] He died in 2013 at the age of 81.[1]
