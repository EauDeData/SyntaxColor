Racing Athletic Club Casablanca (Arabic: نادي الرسينغ الرياضي‎) is a Moroccan football club currently playing in the Botola. The club was founded in 1917 under the name of Racing Athlétic de Casablanca, but in 1969 the name was changed to Association des Douanes Marocains (ADM). In the 1980s the name was changed back to its original format.
