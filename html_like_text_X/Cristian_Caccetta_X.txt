Cristian Caccetta (born 7 August 1986) is an Italian football player. He plays for Paganese.

Club career[edit]
He made his Serie C debut for Foggia on 29 August 2010 in a game against Lucchese.[1]
In October 2014, he was signed by Cosenza after being released by Trapani.[2]
On 29 November 2018, he signed with Sambenedettese.[3]
On 27 July 2019 he signed a one-year contract with Paganese.[4]
