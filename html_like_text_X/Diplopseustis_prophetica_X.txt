Diplopseustis prophetica is a moth in the family Crambidae. It was described by Meyrick in 1887. It is found in Australia, where it has been recorded from Victoria.[1]
The wingspan is about 14 mm. The forewings are pale brownish-ochreous, the base of the costa suffused with dark fuscous. The hindwings are pale whitish-ochreous, becoming pale brownish-ochreous towards the hindmargin.[2]
