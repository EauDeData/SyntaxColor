Kim Ok-ju (born 20 February 1988) is a South Korean field hockey player.[1] At the 2012 Summer Olympics she competed with the Korea women's national field hockey team in the women's tournament.[2] She competed at the 2010 and 2010 Asian Games.[3]
She won a gold medal as a member of the South Korean team at 2014 Asian Games.[4]
