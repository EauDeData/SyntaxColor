Jach'a JawiraEtymologyAymaraLocationCountryBoliviaRegionPotosí Department, Rafael Bustillo Province, Chuqi Uta Municipality
Jach'a Jawira (Aymara jach'a big, great, jawira river,[1] "great river", hispanicized spelling Jachcha Jahuira) is a Bolivian river east of Poopó Lake in the Potosí Department, Rafael Bustillo Province,  Chuqi Uta Municipality.[2]
