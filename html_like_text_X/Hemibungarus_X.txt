Hemibungarus is a genus of venomous elapid snakes known commonly as barred coral snakes.

Distribution[edit]
Hemibungarus species are  endemic to the Philippines.

Species[edit]
Barred coral snake, Hemibungarus calligaster (Wiegmann, 1835)
Hemibungarus gemianulis (Peters, 1872)
