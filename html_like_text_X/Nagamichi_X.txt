Nagamichi (written: 長礼, 長行 or 長訓) is a masculine Japanese given name. Notable people with the name include:

Asano Nagamichi (浅野 長訓, 1812–1872), Japanese daimyō
Nagamichi Kuroda (黒田 長礼, 1889–1978), Japanese ornithologist
Ogasawara Nagamichi (小笠原 長行, 1822–1891), Japanese samurai
