		Karl George Johnston (born 26 February 1979 in Whitby, North Yorkshire) is an English bobsledder who competed for Great Britain at the 2006 Winter Olympics.[1]
At the 2006 Winter Olympics held in Turin, Italy, Johnston competed in the men's four-man bobsleigh in a team that included his brother Lee Johnston. The British team finished in 17th position.[1]
