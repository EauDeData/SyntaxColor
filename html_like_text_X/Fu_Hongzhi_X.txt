Fu Hongzhi



Medal record







Archery


Representing  China


Paralympic Games



 2008 Beijing 
Women's individual recurve - W1/W2

Fu Hongzhi is a Chinese paralympic archer. She won the silver medal at the Women's individual recurve - W1/W2 event at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in Beijing.[1]
