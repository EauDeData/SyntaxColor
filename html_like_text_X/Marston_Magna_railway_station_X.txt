Marston Magna railway station was a railway station that served the village of Marston Magna, Somerset, England from 1857 to 1966 on Heart of Wessex Line.

History[edit]
The station opened in 1857 by the Great Western Railway. The station was closed to both passengers and goods traffic in 1966.[1]
