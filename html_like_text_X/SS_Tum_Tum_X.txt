SS Tum Tum




History
Canada

General characteristics Installed power:
8 hp (6.0 kW)
SS Tum Tum was a Steamship used to move building stone from the granite quarry on the lake shore south of Vernon, BC. She had a total of 8 horsepower. Some speculate she was the Skookum alias Tut Tut with a slight name change as they were similar in number of horsepower.[1]
