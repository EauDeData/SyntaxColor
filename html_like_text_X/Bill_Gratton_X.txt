Bill Gratton (born February 29, 1948) is a Canadian retired professional ice hockey player who played six games in the World Hockey Association with the Calgary Cowboys during the 1975–76 season.[1] He later coached the Paris 29ers, a top tier Canadian Senior ice hockey team in  Major League Hockey.[2]
Gratton was born in Brantford, Ontario.
