Yusuf Ibrahim (born 10 June 1986 in Lagos, Nigeria) is a Nigerian footballer, who currently plays for Air India FC.[1]

Career[edit]
Ibrahim currently plays as a midfielder for Air India FC in the I-League, having signed a deal worth $500.000. Prior to that, he played for Mumbai United.[2]
