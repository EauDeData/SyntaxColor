Nicola Procaccini (born 21 January 1976 in Rome) is an Italian politician and Member of the European Parliament since 2019.[3]
He served as Mayor of Terracina to 30 May 2011 to 6 May 2015. On 21 June 2016 he was again re-elected Mayor of Terracina.
