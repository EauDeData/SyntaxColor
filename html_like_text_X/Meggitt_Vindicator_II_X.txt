The Meggitt Vindicator II, is a Canadian-built Unmanned Aerial Vehicle – Training Target used by the Canadian Forces and United States Navy.[2]
The UAV was used to simulate various types of targets like missiles, aircraft (including helicopter).[3]
The vehicle was not powered and launched via pneumatic catapult.[4]
