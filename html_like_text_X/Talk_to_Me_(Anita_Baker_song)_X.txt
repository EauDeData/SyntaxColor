"Talk to Me" is a 1990 song by American recording artist Anita Baker. The song was released as the lead single in support of her "platinum" selling album, Compositions. "Talk to Me" became a top five R&B hit, peaking at number three on Billboard's Hot Black Singles[1] and number four on Adult Contemporary Songs.[2]
