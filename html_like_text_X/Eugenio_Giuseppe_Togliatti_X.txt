	Eugenio Giuseppe Togliatti

		Eugenio Giuseppe TogliattiBorn(1890-11-03)3 November 1890Orbassano, Piedmont, ItalyDied5 October 1977(1977-10-05) (aged 86)Genoa, Liguria, ItalyNationalityItalianKnown forTogliatti surfacesScientific careerFieldsMathematics

Eugenio Giuseppe Togliatti (3 November 1890 – 5 October 1977) was an Italian mathematician, brother of politician Palmiro Togliatti. He discovered Togliatti surfaces.[1][2][3][4]
