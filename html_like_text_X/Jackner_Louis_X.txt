Jackner Louis (born 15 January 1993) in the Bahamas is a footballer who plays as a Forward. He currently plays for BFA Senior League club Cubs and the Bahamas national football team.

Club career[edit]
In 2011, he signed with BFA Senior League club Dynamos.[1]

International career[edit]
On 2 July 2011 he made his debut for the Bahamas national football team scoring in a 4-0 win over the Turks and Caicos Islands national football team in a 2014 FIFA World Cup qualification – CONCACAF First Round match.[2][3][4]
