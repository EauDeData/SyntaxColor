Tolombeh-ye Nabvi Mohammadabad (Persian: تلمبه نبوي محمداباد‎, also Romanized as Tolombeh-ye Nabvī Moḩammadābād) is a village in Golestan Rural District, in the Central District of Sirjan County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 87, in 22 families.[1]
