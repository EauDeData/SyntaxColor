Kidz in SpaceOriginAuckland, New ZealandGenresHip house, Alternative hip hopLabelsMove The Crowd RecordsWarner Music GroupMembersJosh FountainAshley Hughes
Kidz in Space is a New Zealand pop music and hip-hop band, consisting of Josh Fountain, Ashley Hughes. Their costumed media appearances have been likened to those of band, Split Enz.[1]

Formation[edit]
Hughes and Neshat[who?] knew each other for ten years prior to forming the band, before meeting Fountain. They describe N.E.R.D as one of their main musical influences.[2]
