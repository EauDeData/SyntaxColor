Jin Jian (Chinese: 金坚; pinyin: Jīn Jiān; Mandarin pronunciation: [tɕín tɕjɛ́n]; born 9 March 1994) is a Chinese bobsledder. He competed in the two-man event at the 2018 Winter Olympics.[1]
