Gorno Svilare (Macedonian: Горно Свиларе, Albanian: Sullarë e Epërme) is a village in the municipality of Saraj, Republic of Macedonia.

Demographics[edit]
According to the 2002 census, the village had a total of 712 inhabitants.[1] Ethnic groups in the village include:[1]

Albanians 711
Others 1
