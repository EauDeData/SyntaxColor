Fozeyli (Persian: فضيلي‎, also Romanized as Foẕeylī and Fozeylī; also known as Fizeylī)[1] is a village in Choghamish Rural District, Choghamish District, Dezful County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 848, in 147 families.[2]
