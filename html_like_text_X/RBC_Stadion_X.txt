	RBC Stadion
	
	
		From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
		
		
		
		
		
		Jump to navigation
		Jump to search
		The RBC Stadion, former name among others Vast & Goed Stadion, Rosada Stadion and MariFlex Stadion, is a multi-use stadium in Roosendaal, Netherlands. It is used mostly for football matches. The stadium is able to hold 4,995 people and was built in 2000. It was the home of the club RBC Roosendaal until the club went bankrupt in June 2011. From the 2013‑14 season on it is again the home stadium of RBC now playing as an amateur club.[1]
The stadium is renamed to Herstaco Stadion. It also houses the national football Museum: voetbalexperience.
