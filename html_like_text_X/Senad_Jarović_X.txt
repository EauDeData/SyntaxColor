Senad Jarović (born 20 January 1998) is a Bosnian footballer who plays as a forward for FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce on loan from SønderjyskE.

Club career[edit]
He made his professional debut in the Slovenian PrvaLiga for NK Domžale on 28 February 2016 in a game against NK Krka and scored on his debut.[1]
On 10 February 2019, Jarović signed with SønderjyskE in the Danish Superliga until the summer 2022.[2] On 21 August 2019, he was loaned out to FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce for the rest of 2019.[3]
