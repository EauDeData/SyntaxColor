Mowtowr Pamp-e Shahidi Aqubi (Persian: موتورپم پشهيديعقوبي‎, also Romanized as Mowtowr Pamp-e Shahīdī ʿAqūbī) is a village in Arzuiyeh Rural District, in the Central District of Arzuiyeh County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[1]
