Colaiste na Sceilge is situated in Cahersiveen, County Kerry, Ireland. It is a co-educational community college with an enrolment of 550 students; it grew out of the amalgamation in 1999 of St John Bosco Secondary School, Scoil Uí Chonaill and Waterville Vocational School. In 2018, the former Kerry footballer Maurice Fitzgerald was appointed as its principal.[1]
