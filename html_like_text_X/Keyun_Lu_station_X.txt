Keyun Lu Station (Chinese: 科韵路站; literally: 'Keyun Road Station') is a station on Line 5 of the Guangzhou Metro.[1] It is located at the under the junction of Huangpu Avenue Middle (黄埔大道中) and Keyun Road (科韵路) in the Tianhe District, near Tianhe Science and Technology Park (天河科技园).[2][3][4] It opened on 28 December 2009.[5]
