Amy SniderPersonal informationFull nameMarion Amy SniderNationalityCanadianBorn (1942-05-26) 26 May 1942 (age 77)Toronto, Ontario, CanadaSportSportTrack and fieldEvent(s)80 metres hurdles
Marion Amy Snider (born 26 May 1942) is a Canadian hurdler. She competed in the women's 80 metres hurdles at the 1964 Summer Olympics.[1]
