John Kettleband (22 July 1801 – 3 April 1834) was an English professional cricketer who played first-class cricket from 1826 to 1832.
Kettleband made nine known appearances for the Nottingham Cricket Club in first-class matches, and all of them were against the Sheffield Cricket Club.[1]
