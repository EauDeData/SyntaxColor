Lipowy Las [liˈpɔvɨ ˈlas] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Baranowo, within Ostrołęka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland.[1] It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north of Baranowo, 24 km (15 mi) north-west of Ostrołęka, and 111 km (69 mi) north of Warsaw.
