Malekabad (Persian: ملك اباد‎, also Romanized as Malekābād)[1] is a village in Harirud Rural District, Bujgan District, Torbat-e Jam County, Razavi Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 436, in 89 families.[2]
