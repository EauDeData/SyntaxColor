The badminton men's doubles tournament at the 2016 Summer Olympics took place from 11 to 20 August at Riocentro - Pavilion 4. The seeding was decided on 21 July 2016.[1]

Competition format[edit]
See also: Badminton at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Qualification
The tournament started with a group phase round-robin followed by a knockout stage.

Seeds[edit]
A total of 4 pairs were given seeds.[1]

 Lee Yong-dae / Yoo Yeon-seong (KOR) (Quarter-finals)
 Mohammad Ahsan / Hendra Setiawan (INA) (Group stage)
 Kim Gi-jung / Kim Sa-rang (KOR) (Quarter-finals)
 Fu Haifeng / Zhang Nan (CHN) (Gold medalists)
