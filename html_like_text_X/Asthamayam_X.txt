AsthamayamDirected byP. ChandrakumarProduced byMadhuWritten bySarah ThomasStarringMadhuJayanSharadaJayabharathiMusic byShyamEdited byG. VenkittaramanProductioncompany Uma Arts  Distributed byUma ArtsRelease date

CountryIndiaLanguageMalayalam
Asthamayam is a 1978 Indian Malayalam film, directed by P. Chandrakumar and produced by Madhu. The film stars Madhu, Jayan, Sharada and Jayabharathi in the lead roles. The film has musical score by Shyam.[1][2][3]
