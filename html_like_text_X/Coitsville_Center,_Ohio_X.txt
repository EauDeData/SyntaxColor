		Coitsville Center is an unincorporated community in Mahoning County, in the U.S. state of Ohio.

History[edit]
A variant name was Coitsville.[1] A post office called Coitsville was established in 1826, and remained in operation until 1900.[2] The community was named for its location near the geographical center of Coitsville Township.[3]
