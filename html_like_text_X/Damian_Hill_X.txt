Damian Hill (~1976 - 2018) was an Australian actor.[1] He was the writer of and an actor in Pawno for which he received two 2016 AACTA Award nominations, Best Actor in a Leading Role and Best Original Screenplay.[2] He was also nominated for Best Actor in 2018 for West of Sunshine.[3]
