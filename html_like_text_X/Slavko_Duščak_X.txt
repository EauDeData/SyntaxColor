Slavko Duščak (born January 2, 1974 in Ljubljana) is a Slovenian former professional basketball player and a basketball coach.

Daneu played for Slovan, Olimpija, Pivovarna Laško, Krka, Teramo Basket, Elektra Esotech, Anwil Włocławek, Soproni KC and Koper.[1]

Slovenian national team[edit]
Kraljević was a member of the Slovenia national basketball team since 1995.  He competed at Eurobasket 2003. He represented Slovenia officially at 29 games [2] and scored 140 points.[3]
