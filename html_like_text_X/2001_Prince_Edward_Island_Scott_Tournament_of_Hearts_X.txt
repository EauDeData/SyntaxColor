The 2001 Prince Edward Island Scott Tournament of Hearts was held January 19–23  at the Charlottetown Curling Club in Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island. The winning team was Team Shelly Bradley who represented Prince Edward Island, finished with a 7-4 round-robin record, losing the tiebreaker for a place in the semi-finals at the 2001 Scott Tournament of Hearts in Sudbury, Ontario.[1]
