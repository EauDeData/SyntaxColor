Nongdananlu station[1] (simplified Chinese: 农大南路站; traditional Chinese: 農大南路站; pinyin: Nóngdànánlù Zhàn; literally: 'Nongda (Agricultural University) South Road station') is a station on the Line 16 of the Beijing Subway, located southwest of the west campus of China Agricultural University. It was opened on 30 December 2017.
