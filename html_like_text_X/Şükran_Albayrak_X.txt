Şükran Albayrak (born 12 May 1982 in Kartal, İstanbul) is a Turkish TV presenter, sport hostess, and retired professional female basketball player.
1.86 m (6 ft 1 in) tall at 74 kg (163 lb), she played as forward for Fenerbahçe Istanbul and Turkish National team between 1992 and 2006.[1][2][3]
Albayrak co-presents "Sport Center", a sports news program at the sportstv every weekday evening.[3][4]
