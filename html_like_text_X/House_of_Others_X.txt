House of Others (Georgian: სხვისი სახლი) is a 2016 Georgian drama film directed by Rusudan Glurjidze.[1][2][3]
It was selected as the Georgian entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 89th Academy Awards but it was not nominated.[4][5][6]
