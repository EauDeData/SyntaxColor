Norberto Orejel Ochoa (born December 21, 1990 in Riverside, California) is an American soccer player who plays for Moreno Valley Futbol Club of the United Premier Soccer League.

Career[edit]
College and Amateur[edit]
Ochoa began playing college soccer at Mt. San Antonio College in 2011 and 2012.[1] Following two years in San Antonio, Ochoa spent time in Europe with both SC Rheindorf Altach and Club Brugge.[2]
Ochoa also spent one year as a senior at UC Riverside.[3]

Professional[edit]
Ochoa signed with United Soccer League side Charlotte Independence on March 24, 2016.[4]
