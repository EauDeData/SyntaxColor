Chandarlapadu is a village in Krishna district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is located in Chandarlapadu mandal of Vijayawada revenue division.[3][4] It is one of the villages in the mandal to be a part of Andhra Pradesh Capital Region.[5]
