	Come On Girl (Bert Berns song)
	
	
		From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
		
		
		
		
		
		Jump to navigation
		Jump to search
		"Come On Girl" is a 1963 song written by Bert Berns and Stanley Kahan, credited as "Bob Elgin, Bert Berns and Kay Rogers".[1] It was the final single for The Jarmels in 1963,[2] and was then covered by The Redcaps 1963. The Knight Brothers covered the song in 1964.
