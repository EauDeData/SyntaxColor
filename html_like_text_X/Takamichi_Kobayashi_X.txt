Takamichi Kobayashi (小林 高道, Kobayashi Takamichi, born January 3, 1979) is a former Japanese football player.

Playing career[edit]
Kobayashi was born in Niigata Prefecture on January 3, 1979. After graduating from high school, he joined Regional Leagues club Albirex Niigata based in his local in 1997. The club was promoted to Japan Football League from 1998 and J2 League from 1999. Although he played several matches as midfielder every season, he could not play many matches. He retired end of 2001 season.
