Manuel Garcia is a census-designated place (CDP) in Starr County, Texas, United States. It is a new CDP formed from part of the former La Casita-Garciasville CDP prior to the 2010 census with a population of 203.[1]

Geography[edit]
Manuel Garcia is located at 26°20′1″N 98°42′6″W﻿ / ﻿26.33361°N 98.70167°W﻿ / 26.33361; -98.70167 (26.333654, -98.701643).[2]
