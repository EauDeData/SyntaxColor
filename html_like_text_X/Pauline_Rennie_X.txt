	Pauline Rennie

		For the fictional character, see Pauline Rennie (Under the Dome).
Pauline Rennie (born January 4, 1945) is a Canadian voice actress whose career included voice roles on The Care Bears.[1] Rennie, who hails from Toronto,  provided the voice of Fizgerald Fieldmouse on Maggie Muggins,[2] as well as voices on Mary Grannan's follow-up 1960 series for the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation, Just Mary.[3]

Roles[edit]
Maggie Muggins (1955)
Just Mary (1960)
The Care Bears Movie (1985) - Grams Bear, Cozy Heart Penguin
Star Wars: Ewoks (1985-1986)
Popples (1986) - Pancake
The Raccoons (1987) - Aunt Gertie
The Care Bears (1985-1988) - Grams Bear, Cozy Heart Penguin, Treat Heart Pig
