Asyifa LatiefBornAsyifa Syafiningdyah Putriambami Latief (1988-09-20) 20 September 1988 (age 31)Bandung, IndonesiaEducationParahyangan Catholic University, Bandung, West JavaTitleMiss Indonesia 2010
Asyifa Syafiningdyah Putrambami Latief (born 20 September 1988), better known as Asyifa Latief, is an Indonesian model who was Miss Indonesia 2010.[1] She was the first Arab-Indonesian to be crowned as Miss Indonesia.
Latief represented Indonesia at the Miss World 2010 competition, which was held in November 2010.
Latief was also crowned as Miss Best Body.[2] She is a model and a student at Parahyangan Catholic University at Bandung, majoring in accounting.[3]

Miss Indonesia's CROWN[edit]
Asyifa Latief is successor incumbent at 1 June 2010 - 3 June 2011 (only 1 year hold the crown) and her crown is given to Miss Indonesia 2011.
