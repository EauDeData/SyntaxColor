Pendle Vale College is a mixed 11 to 16 comprehensive school in Nelson, Lancashire.

History[edit]
The school opened in September 2006, as part of the first wave of Building Schools for the Future. It occupied the site of the former Walton High School. The new buildings were completed in September 2008.
Pendle Vale College's facilities include facilities for ICT, a sporting centre, rock climbing wall, multi gym and all weather pitches, music studios, and dance and drama suites.
