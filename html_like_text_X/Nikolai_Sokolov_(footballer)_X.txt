Nikolai Yevgrafovich Sokolov (Russian: Николай Евграфович Соколов) (May 12, 1897 in Moscow – December 15, 1988 in Razliv, Leningrad Oblast) was a Soviet football player.

Honours[edit]
RSFSR champion: 1920, 1922.
USSR champion: 1920.
International career[edit]
Sokolov made his debut for USSR on November 16, 1924 in a friendly against Turkey.
