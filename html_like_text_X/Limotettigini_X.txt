Limotettigini is a tribe of leafhoppers in the family Cicadellidae. There are at least 2 genera and about 11 described species in Limotettigini.[1]

Genera[edit]
These two genera belong to the tribe Limotettigini:

Frigartus Oman, 1949 g b
Limotettix Sahlberg, 1871 c g b
